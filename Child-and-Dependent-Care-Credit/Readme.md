## What this decision tree covers

### 1. Identify a qualifying person

The diagram walks through the common categories of qualifying persons, including:

- Qualifying child
- Spouse
- Other dependent
- Certain individuals who would qualify as a dependent except for specific IRS tests

### 2. Identify qualifying care expenses

Examples include:

- Daycare or childcare
- Before- and after-school care
- Summer day camp
- Babysitter or nanny
- Adult day care
- In-home caregiver
- Home health aide
- Care for an elderly parent or disabled dependent
- Other work-related care expenses
